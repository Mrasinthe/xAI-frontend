XAI

XAI is a field of artificial intelligence (AI) that focuses on making AI systems more transparent and understandable to humans. AI models, particularly deep learning models, can be highly complex and difficult to interpret.

This lack of transparency can lead to issues of trust, accountability, and ethics. XAI aims to address these concerns by providing insights into how AI models make decisions. It enables users to understand the underlying logic and factors that contribute to AI predictions or recommendations.

Fairness

Fairness in AI refers to the ethical principle that AI systems should treat all individuals or groups fairly and without bias. AI systems can inadvertently perpetuate or even exacerbate existing biases and inequalities present in society.

Fairness is a desired quality of a system requiring it to avoid discrimination by ensuring that the system outcomes treat people in an equivalent manner. In machine learning, fairness is evaluated by comparing the model performance with the protected and unprotected attributes.
